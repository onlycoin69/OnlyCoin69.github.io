The White Paper

1. Introduction

The OnlyCoin ($OFC) project is a community-driven initiative aimed at revolutionizing the concept of banking by creating a "good bank" that prioritizes community building and social good. The project seeks to leverage the power of blockchain technology to create a transparent, decentralized, and inclusive financial ecosystem. OnlyCoin will serve as the native token of this ecosystem, empowering community members and driving the project's philanthropic goals.

2. Goals

The primary goal of OnlyCoin is to build a community-centric "good bank" that brings people together. This will be achieved through several key objectives:

  • **Community Empowerment:** To provide financial tools and services that are accessible and beneficial to all community members, regardless of their background or economic status.
  • **Social Impact:** To use a portion of the project's resources to support community initiatives, charitable causes, and projects that align with the "good bank" philosophy.
  • **Transparency and Trust:** To operate with complete transparency, with all transactions and decisions recorded on the blockchain, fostering trust and accountability within the community.
  • **Financial Inclusion:** To create a platform that is easy to use and understand, breaking down barriers to financial participation and empowering individuals to take control of their financial futures.

3. Tokenomics ($OFC)

The OnlyCoin ($OFC) token will have a fixed supply and its distribution will be as follows:

  • **50% for Contest Giveaways:** A significant portion of the tokens will be allocated for contests and giveaways, rewarding active community participation and incentivizing engagement. This will be a key mechanism for distributing tokens and growing the community.
  • **30% for Liquidity:** This allocation will be used to ensure a healthy and stable market for the $OFC token. Providing ample liquidity is crucial for enabling easy trading and price stability.
  • **15% for CEX Reserves:** These tokens will be reserved for future listings on centralized exchanges (CEXs). This will increase the token's accessibility and market reach.
  • **5% to the Team:** A small percentage of tokens will be allocated to the founding team. This allocation will be subject to a vesting schedule to ensure long-term commitment to the project's success and to align the team's interests with those of the community.

4. Conclusion

OnlyCoin ($OFC) is more than just a cryptocurrency; it is a movement towards a more equitable and community-focused financial system. By building a "good bank" that prioritizes people over profits and by leveraging a transparent tokenomics model, OnlyCoin aims to create a lasting positive impact on its community and beyond.
